Project Overview

Google Suite, Miro Figma & Descript
A new meal plan feature on the H-E-B mobile app, which allows users to find recipes with ingredients sold at H-E-B, add them to their meal plan through the app, and buy the ingredients.

What I Did

This was a highly collaborative effort between myself and 2 other UX designers. Each team member was involved with every step of the process at varying capacities.

The Problem

Young adults who want to eat healthier and save money are lacking resources that would give them more options for a balanced, home cooked and varied diet.

Who, What & Why?

The information gathered by the user interviews was grouped by category and analyzed via affinity diagram. This was used to create a user persona and story board to firmly establish the direction

Final Thoughts

Our hope is that H-E-B customers will never end up missing an ingredient for their favorite recipe ever again! We believe that integrating your cookbook and your shopping cart is the future, and we are working towards making that happen. With further rounds of testing and iteration, we aim to make this a reality.
